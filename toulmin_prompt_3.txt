"Claim": The main argument that demonstrates the commenter's stance (showing their agreement or disagrrement). Do NOT include extra suggestions here.
"Data": The factual evidence like statistics, expert opinions, examples, or other forms of support that justify the claim."Warrant": The logical connection between the claim and the data.
For our purposes, we only need the main Claim, the Data that support it, and how these two are related (ergo the “Warrant”). If there’s no data, write “None”. Remember that we only need the data that are included in the claim, not the one in the suggestions.
